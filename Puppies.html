This is a page about puppies. Here you will find photos of German Shepards, Labradoodles, Greyhounds, and more. All the best types of puppies.

German Shephards. Fun Fact: The breed was standardized in Germany in the mid 1800s where they were working herding sheep.

Labradoodles. Fun Fact: Labradoodles were originally bred in Australia to create a guide dog that with a loyal demeanor and non-sheeding coat.

Greyhounds. Fun Fact: At a greyhounds maximum race speed, they can run a speed of 43 miles per hour.

Australian Shepards. Fun Fact: Despite the name, Australian Shepards originated in the U.S., not Australia, around the time of the Gold Rush in the 1840s.

Golden Retrievers. Fun Fact: Golden Retrievers were developed in Scotland in the mid-1800s, orginally bred to hunt fowl.

Huskies. Fun Fact: Huskies are the mascot of the best University in the world.
